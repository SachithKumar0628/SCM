Amity University Bengaluru 
(AUB) is a leading private university in Karnataka, offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including engineering, business administration,
law, and computer applications.
Admissions are based on merit and entrance exams such as JEE-Mains, CAT, MAT, and Amity's own AMCAT. The university also provides scholarships up to 100% for eligible students
